# Product

## Register

brand

## Platform

web

## Users

Food production managers — production chiefs, technologists, and quality/HACCP managers at processing plants, factories, and food-production facilities in Russia. They land on this page while solving a compliance problem: equipping (or re-equipping) their facility with cleaning inventory that satisfies ХАССП requirements and passes inspections. They are practical, regulation-literate buyers who evaluate suppliers on competence and delivery reliability, not marketing.

## Product Purpose

АНКУВЕР supplies professional HACCP (ХАССП) color-coded cleaning inventory for the food industry and audits facilities for compliance. The site is a lead-generation landing page; success means a visitor leaves their contact details to receive the PDF catalog. The KP (commercial proposal) request is the hotter, lower-volume secondary conversion.

## Positioning

Speed and stock: everything is in stock and ships within 24 hours, with recurring resupply on schedule. One supplier who always delivers, fast — that is the claim every section reinforces.

## Conversion & proof

- Primary CTA: получить каталог инвентаря (leave contact, receive the PDF catalog). Secondary CTA: запросить коммерческое предложение for visitors ready to describe their facility.
- The line a visitor remembers after 10 seconds: «Оснащаем пищевые производства под ключ».
- Belief ladder: (1) АНКУВЕР understands ХАССП and my industry → (2) they carry everything my facility needs → (3) they deliver fast and without failures → (4) requesting the catalog commits me to nothing.
- Proof on hand: real client testimonials and product/facility photos exist and will replace the current placeholders — drop them into `.impeccable/assets/proof/`. Client names/logos are not yet cleared for publication.

## Brand Personality

Engineering expert: strict, procedural, industrial. The brand speaks in zones, regulations, and checklists, and should feel like a competent auditor walking the client's facility — precise and confident, never salesy. The 5-color HACCP zone coding is the brand's native vocabulary.

## Anti-references

Not a wholesale price-list site: no cluttered product grids, popups, or «Купить оптом!» banners. Not a flashy startup landing: no gradient-heavy SaaS aesthetics that read unserious to industrial buyers.

## Design Principles

- **Zone coding is language, not decoration.** The 5-color HACCP system appears wherever it carries real meaning (zones, product groups, the logomark) and nowhere else.
- **Speak like an auditor.** Copy is procedural and concrete — checklists, zone names, regulation terms — because precision itself is the proof of competence.
- **Prove stock, not promises.** Specific numbers (24 часа, со склада, по графику) and real facility photos over adjectives and stock imagery.
- **Every ask is zero-risk.** CTAs are framed as no-obligation requests; the visitor gives little and gets the catalog.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA: body text at ≥4.5:1 contrast, full keyboard navigation, and reduced-motion alternatives for all animation (already started with the existing `prefers-reduced-motion` rules).
